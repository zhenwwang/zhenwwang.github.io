Research

My research interest lies in the areas of computer graphics, computer vision, and human-computer interaction. Recently, I am working on the following topics:

  • Image editing & generation in the context of data-driven graphic design
  • 3D assets generation from multi-modal inputs (text, image, 3D exemplar)

    		• Language-based Photo Color Adjustment for Graphic Designs
    Zhenwei Wang, Nanxuan Zhao, Gerhard Hancke, Rynson W.H. Lau,
    ACM Trans. on Graphics (Proc. ACM SIGGRAPH 2023), August 2023
    project page / paper / arXiv / video

    We present LangRecol, a new language-based approach for recoloring photos in graphic designs. LangRecol uses deep networks to predict user-specified source colors and target objects/regions and then recolors the photo via a semantic-palette-based photo recoloring module.
