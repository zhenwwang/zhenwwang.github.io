I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Rynson W.H. Lau. Before that, I was a research intern at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), mentored by Min Yang. I received my B.Eng. degree from Xiamen University.

My research interest lies in the areas of computer graphics, computer vision/machine learning, and human-computer interaction. Recently I am interested in image editing & generation in the context of AI-driven graphic design..
